Reading: Strange Traditions

While many people know about Santa Claus, other countries have stranger traditions. In Austria, bad children worry about 'Krampus'. He is a scary, horned monster who punishes naughty kids during Christmas time.


In Spain, there is a tradition called 'La Tomatina'. It is a huge festival where people throw thousands of tomatoes at each other just for fun! It happens in August, so it is very messy and hot.


In Denmark, people save their old plates and dishes all year round. On New Year's Eve, they throw them at the front doors of their friends' houses. A big pile of broken glass at your door means you have many friends!
