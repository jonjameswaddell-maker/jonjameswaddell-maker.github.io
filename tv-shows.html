üìñ Reading: The Golden Age

The Changing Face of TV

Twenty years ago, families sat around the TV at 7:00 PM to watch the news or their favorite comedy series. If you missed it, you missed it! Today, things are very different. Streaming services like Netflix and Disney+ have changed our habits completely.

Now, we can "binge-watch" an entire season of a wildlife documentary or a crime drama in one weekend. We don't have to wait for the adverts to finish to make a cup of tea. However, some people think we have lost something. The shared experience of watching a huge sports event or a reality show finale live is becoming rare.
