üìñ Reading: The 15-Minute Rule

The 15-Minute Rule

Many people hate cleaning their homes because it feels overwhelming. They wait until Saturday morning and spend four hours cleaning everything. This is exhausting!

However, there is a better way. It is called the "15-Minute Rule". The idea is simple: you set a timer for 15 minutes every evening. During this time, you do as much cleaning as you can. You might load the dishwasher, sweep the kitchen floor, or put away toys. When the timer stops, you stop!

This method keeps the house tidy without making you tired. It helps you build a habit. Psychologists say that doing a little bit every day reduces stress and makes your home a happier place.
